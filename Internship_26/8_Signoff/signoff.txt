====================================================================
STAGE 8: SIGNOFF & GDSII STREAMOUT (CADENCE INNOVUS)
====================================================================
This folder contains:
- Setup/Hold Signoff Timing Reports
- Geometry, DRC, Antenna, and Connectivity Reports
- Final Signoff Netlists, SDC, SDF, and SPEF
- Signoff Layout Screenshot

Tools Used:
- Cadence Innovus

Workflow:
1. Ran full signoff-mode static timing analysis for both setup and
   hold, capturing final timing margins after all post-route
   optimization.
2. Ran final geometry verification, DRC, process-antenna checking,
   and full-design connectivity checking with tightened error/warning
   limits appropriate for tape-out signoff.
3. Generated the final signoff deliverables: the post-route netlist
   (with and without power/ground), the signoff SDC, a signoff SDF for
   back-annotated gate-level simulation, and an SPEF file for parasitic
   extraction.
4. Streamed out the final GDSII using a 6-metal-layer (M1-M5 + TOP_M)
   layer map, completing the digital implementation flow.
5. Generated a consolidated set of design-health, constraint,
   coverage, and detailed timing (GBA and PBA) reports for final
   documentation.

Learning Outcomes:
- Learned the distinction between optimization-time timing checks and
  final signoff-mode STA, and why signoff numbers are the ones that
  matter for tape-out.
- Understood how DRC, antenna, and connectivity checks at signoff
  differ in strictness from earlier-stage checks.
- Learned the purpose of each final deliverable (netlist, SDC, SDF,
  SPEF, GDSII) in the handoff from digital implementation to
  fabrication.
