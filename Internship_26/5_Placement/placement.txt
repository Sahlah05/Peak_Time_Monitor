====================================================================
STAGE 5: PLACEMENT (CADENCE INNOVUS)
====================================================================
This folder contains:
- Cell/Pin Density and Congestion Maps
- Pre-CTS Timing Reports
- Pre-CTS Netlist
- Placement Layout Screenshot

Tools Used:
- Cadence Innovus

Workflow:
1. Configured placement mode with a high global congestion effort and
   a maximum global placement density of 0.8, without concurrent I/O
   pin placement or clock-gate-aware placement.
2. Ran place_opt_design to perform global placement, legalization,
   detailed placement, and in-place optimization in a single pass,
   then verified legality with checkPlace.
3. Generated cell density, pin density, and routing congestion maps
   (20x20 grid) to identify hotspots before committing to CTS.
4. Ran an iterative setup-timing check/optimize loop
   (report_timing -> optDesign -preCTS) up to four times, only
   invoking optimization when worst slack fell below a 0.2 ns
   threshold.
5. Saved the pre-CTS netlist (with and without power/ground) and SDC
   for hand-off into clock tree synthesis.

Learning Outcomes:
- Learned how global placement density and congestion effort settings
  trade off routability against cell packing.
- Understood how density/congestion maps are used to catch local
  hotspots before they become routing violations.
- Learned why pre-CTS timing closure is checked iteratively rather
  than assumed, since ideal (zero-skew) clock timing at this stage is
  optimistic compared to post-CTS reality.
