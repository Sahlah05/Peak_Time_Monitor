STAGE 7: ROUTING (CADENCE INNOVUS - NANOROUTE)
====================================================================
This folder contains:
- Post-Route Connectivity/Congestion Reports
- Routing Layout Screenshot

Tools Used:
- Cadence Innovus (NanoRoute detailed router)

Workflow:
1. Configured NanoRoute for timing-driven and signal-integrity (SI)
   aware routing, with the timing/congestion trade-off effort set to
   favor congestion resolution.
2. Enabled automatic antenna diode insertion using the design's
   antenna repair cells so that long routed nets would not damage
   thin-oxide transistor gates during fabrication.
3. Ran routeDesign in global+detail mode with via and wire
   optimization enabled, completing full global and detailed routing
   of all signal and clock nets in a single pass.
4. Checked routing connectivity and post-route congestion to confirm
   no open nets, dislocated terminals, or unresolved hotspots
   remained.

Learning Outcomes:
- Learned how timing-driven and SI-driven routing settings influence
  net ordering and shielding decisions during detailed routing.
- Understood the purpose of antenna diode insertion as a
  manufacturing reliability safeguard against plasma-induced gate
  damage.
- Learned how post-route congestion and connectivity reports validate
  that the routed design is both electrically complete and physically
  clean.
